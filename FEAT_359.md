# Collaborative Legal Research - Issue #359

## Overview
Enable legal teams to collaborate on research with real-time document sharing, annotations, and version control.

## Components
1. **Document Sharing**: Share research documents across team
2. **Real-Time Collaboration**: Simultaneous editing and annotations
3. **Annotation System**: Add notes, highlights, comments
4. **Version Control**: Track document changes
5. **Access Control**: Role-based document permissions

## Benefits
✅ Team collaboration on research
✅ Real-time annotations
✅ Document version history
✅ Shared research workspace
✅ Improved efficiency
